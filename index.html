About Premier Roofing

Founded in 2000, Premier Roofing emerged from a vision to revolutionize Calgary's roofing industry. What began as a small family operation has grown into one of the city's most trusted roofing contractors, built on a foundation of excellence, integrity, and innovation. Our journey has been marked by continuous learning, adaptation to new technologies, and an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction.

Our mission is to provide Calgary homeowners and businesses with roofing solutions that combine durability, aesthetics, and environmental responsibility. We believe in building not just roofs, but relationships, treating every project with the same level of care and attention we would give to our own homes. Our core values of transparency, craftsmanship, and community service guide every decision we make and every nail we drive.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical timeline for a roof replacement?

Most residential roofing projects are completed within 2-3 days, depending on your roof's size and complexity. We provide a detailed timeline during consultation and keep you updated throughout the process. Weather permitting, we maintain strict scheduling to minimize disruption to your daily routine.

What types of warranty do you offer?

We provide a comprehensive 25-year warranty on materials and a 10-year warranty on workmanship. This covers potential issues with installation, materials, and water penetration. Additionally, we offer annual maintenance programs to extend your roof's lifespan and maintain warranty validity.

How do you handle unexpected weather during installation?

We closely monitor weather forecasts and have emergency protocols in place. Our teams are equipped with industrial-grade temporary covering systems to protect your home if weather conditions change. We never compromise on quality or safety due to weather and will reschedule if conditions are unsuitable.

What financing options are available?

We partner with several financial institutions to offer flexible payment plans, including 0% interest for 12 months on qualifying projects. We also work with all major insurance companies and can assist with claims processing for storm damage repairs.

How do you ensure quality control?

Our multi-point inspection process includes pre-installation assessment, daily progress checks, and final inspection by a senior supervisor. We use advanced imaging technology to detect potential issues and document every phase of installation. All our work meets or exceeds Calgary building codes and manufacturer specifications.
